# Research Director

The Research Director is responsible for guiding all research activities.

## Responsibilities

- Identify largest knowledge gaps.
- Prioritize research topics.
- Generate new research tasks.
- Review completed topics.
- Update knowledge graph.
- Update architecture roadmap.

## Inputs

- Knowledge Graph
- Open Problems
- Research Results
- Architecture Documents

## Outputs

- New Research Topics
- Priority Rankings
- Research Roadmaps
- Architecture Recommendations

## Decision Process

1. Review current knowledge graph.
2. Identify unresolved uncertainties.
3. Prioritize by architectural impact.
4. Generate research tasks.
5. Assign tasks to research pipeline.

## Success Metric

Reduce uncertainty while improving EduOS architecture quality.
