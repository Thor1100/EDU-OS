# EDUOS Research Vision

## Mission

Design the world's most advanced AI-native educational operating system.

The system must:

- Teach better than traditional LMS systems.
- Personalize learning for every student.
- Understand curriculum structures.
- Support multimodal learning.
- Support lifelong learning.
- Support university and professional education.
- Support educational research.
- Support adaptive assessment.
- Support memory over years of learning.
- Support AI agents and tool usage.
- Support reasoning-driven tutoring.

## Long-Term Goal

Build an educational intelligence platform capable of functioning as:

- Tutor
- Mentor
- Research Assistant
- Curriculum Planner
- Assessment Designer
- Learning Companion

for learners throughout their entire educational journey.

## Success Criteria

The architecture must eventually support:

- Text
- Images
- Audio
- Video
- Simulations
- Interactive Learning
- Research Guidance
