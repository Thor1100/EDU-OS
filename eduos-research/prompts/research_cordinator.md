# Research Coordinator

Process exactly ONE research topic.

Workflow:

1. Read queue/active.md
2. Identify active topic
3. Launch Educational Researcher
4. Launch Critic
5. Launch Systems Architect
6. Generate ADR
7. Generate Future Questions
8. Update queue
9. Stop

Required Deliverables:

* summary.md
* critique.md
* architecture_impact.md
* ADR
* future_questions.md

Never process multiple topics simultaneously.

Stop after completing one topic.
